Know more about the App.

This app was created by Paulo C. Nazareno, a third year Information Technology student studying at the University of Santo Tomas with the intention of helping people find a suitable doctor to see in the nearest place near them. This app not only lets people find the doctor they need but also allows people to request an appointment with the chosen doctor by filling up the form provided in the app.
